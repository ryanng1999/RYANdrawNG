Hey there!




As a neurodiverse artist based in Queens, I have been very passionate about art since age three. Art was my first vehicle of communication. My preferred mediums are pencils and watercolor, but I am continuously inspired to learn about and try many different forms of art through my visits to art museums and volunteer experience at Materials for the Arts. At first, I used Weebly to build my art website. After graduating with an Associate in Applied Science Degree in Computer Engineering Technology and a Certificate of Completion in Front-End Web Development, I wanted to apply my coding skills to rebuilding my art website.
